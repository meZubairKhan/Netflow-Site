Connectivity Services

Netflow’s DataCom services provide comprehensive solutions for efficient and secure data communication, reliable connectivity, high-speed data transfer, and seamless integration across multiple locations. We play a vital role in supporting business operations, enhancing productivity, and enabling effective communication in today’s interconnected world.

Metro Ethernet

Netflow’s metronet is a hyper-speed metro area optical backbone service, enabling carrier-grade Fiber To The Premises (FTTP) based communication for businesses to achieve unparalleled capacity services beyond gigabits.

IP MPLs

Netflow’s IP MPLS service provides reliable interconnection for geographically dispersed sites. As a carrier-grade alternative to traditional leased line service, it leverages the fiber optic network to offer a reliable networking solution for multi-site connectivity services, backed by QoS, strong SLAs, protocols, and traffic engineering.

Global MPLs

Whether you are a Pakistani enterprise looking to expand your operations globally or an international enterprise seeking to establish a business in Pakistan, Netflow‘s global MPLS is your gateway to connectivity. With over 20 years of data network industry experience in serving regional and international customers, we understand your preferences and challenges. We will assist you in seamlessly integrating your WANs across boundaries, making them feel connected as if they were located in your own city.

IP VPNs

If you need to connect one or more offices or enable multiple home or field workers to access your systems via our secure remote access capability, Netflow‘s IP VPN is your answer. It uses the internet to bring voice, data, and video together on a single network. Irrespective of the location of your staff, they’ll be able to access resources as if they were in the same office.

IP Transit

Netflow‘s IP transit offers the most reliable means to route IP traffic over the internet, providing an effective service by leveraging high bandwidth technologies.

IPLC

Netflow’s IPLC service offers a variety of solutions at different speeds, ranging from E1 to 10G. These circuits enable the transmission of large volumes of data swiftly, securely, and efficiently. Netflow provides seamless connectivity on major submarine cable systems via SMW 5, SMW 4, SMW 3, IMEWE, TW1, and AAE1

DPLC

Netflow’s DPLC product platform is a 100% premium quality optical fiber connection serving a varied customer base. Our service provisioning options include high speed data transmission, audio and video conferencing, as well as any other corporate communication needs.

Carrier Last Mile

Netflow‘s carrier last mile is a connectivity portfolio for customers seeking carrier-grade connectivity across Pakistan. Backed by master service agreements, strong SLAs and partner relationship management, carrier last mile is an agile and competitive solution available off the shelf to the telcos and data operators, enabling reach and business opportunities.

IPT Wholesale

Netflow’s carrier business presents a range of high-capacity subscription and wholesale bulk capacity choices for operators, scalable from Mbps to Gbps. These options include FE and GE with electrical or optical handoff.

IRU and Fiber

Netflow offers transmission, IP core, and long haul infrastructure assets and capacity on long-term lease agreements. Operators have the opportunity to access comprehensive network coverage or bridge network gaps throughout Pakistan, enabling them to effectively meet the exponential data demands and reach. The services include complete operational and network outsourcing, linked to the provision of readily available fiber infrastructure to support both network and business expansion.

Tele Housing

Netflow’s long haul network extends telehousing and colocation facilities across its transmission sites throughout Pakistan. Carriers and operators have the advantage of locally hosting and distributing their network components, resulting in enhanced economies of scale and serviceability.
